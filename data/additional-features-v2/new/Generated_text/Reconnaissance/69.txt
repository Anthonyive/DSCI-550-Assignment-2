The Local Government Contract
The Local Government Contract (LGC) is a contract that is (a) executed by a local government agency/labor of the country and (b) is executed by a firm or an institution of the country.
The contract is executed by a firm or an institution of the country (i.e. a foreign government).
Therefore, as the contract is executed by a firm or an institution of the country, it is that the firm or institution will write the contract into the contract book and instruct the contractor on the procedures to be followed in their execution.
This is the same procedure that the Local Government Contract (LGC) has been executed for. This contract is due on 30th of October, 2015.

The contractor had to be well prepared to pay the total sum.
The contract is being executed by a local government agency/labor of the country.
Therefore, this contract is still in the nature of the contract.
Therefore, the contractor is still under charge of the contract.
